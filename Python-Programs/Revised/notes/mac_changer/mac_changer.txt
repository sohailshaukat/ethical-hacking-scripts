eth0: ethernet
wlan0: wifi

Mac_address is a unique address allocated to every personal computer. MAC stands for media access control.
It is Permanent, Physical and Unique.
It is assigned to network interfaces by device manufacuturer and is device specific. (netword interface device.)
It is a 12 digit hexadecimal string seperated by ':'.
For e.g:
    00:11:22:33:44:55

Every mac has source mac and destination mac.

MAC is used to identify devices, hence changing it helps with Anonymity.
Changing MAC can also be used to impersonate other device and bypass filters.

How to change MAC Address:
    
A. Disable the interface:
    ifconfig "interface" down
B. Modify it's option:
    ifconfig "interface" hw ether "new_mac_addr"
C. Enable the interface:
    ifconfig "interface" up

e.g: 
    ifconfig wlan0 down
    ifconfig wlan0 hw ehter "00:11:22:33:44:55" 
    ifconfig wlan0 up
